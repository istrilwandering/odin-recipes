Chicken & Broccoli

Description

A perennial family-favorite, Chicken and Broccoli casserole is sure to please kids and adults alike. When served over rice, this combination of tender chicken, broccoli, creamy sauce, and melted cheese serves as a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs cooked, cubed (or shredded) chicken
  • 24 oz frozed broccoli florets
  • 2 10.5 oz cans Cream of Chicken soup
  • 1 10.5 oz can Cream of Mushroom soup
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • salt and pepper
  • shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Place the frozen broccoli in a 9x13 glass baking dish.
  3. Layer the cooked chicken on top of the broccoli.
  4. In a bowl, combine the cans of soup, mayonnaise, curry powder, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour soup mixture over the chicken.
  6. Bake for 30-40 minutes until hot in the center.
  7. Top with shredded cheddar cheese, then bake for an additional 10 minutes to allow cheese to melt.
  8. Serve over cooked rice.
