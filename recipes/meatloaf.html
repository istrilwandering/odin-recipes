Meatloaf

Description

A family staple for many years, this meatloaf comes together quickly for an easy weeknight meal. Pair with your favorite vegetable and potato preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 lbs lean ground beef
  • 1 packet onion soup mix
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1 tsp worchestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp dry mustard
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a small bowl, combine bread crumbs, eggs, and milk, and mix well.
  3. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumb mixture, onion soup mix, ketchup, worchestershire sauce, mustard, salt, and pepper and mix well.
  4. In a 9x13 glass baking dish, shape the ground beef mixture into a loaf shape.
  5. Bake for 1 hour, or until a meat thermometer inserted into center of the loaf registers 165 degrees F.
  6. Let stand 10 minutes, then slice and serve.
